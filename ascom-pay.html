My Recipe Diary

What is the project? - An app where individuals keep track of their favorite recipes, cooking experiments, and culinary experiences.
What is the MVP? - The Mvp is an app that collect data (Name of Meal, Ingredients in input order and quantity of each Ingredientsto be added) fron the user, The User can Create, Read, Edit and Delete or inputed data
What are the sprinkles? - The sprinkles are the following User Generated Content, Intuitive and User-Friendly Interface, Recipe Customization and Recipe Sharing.
